Description:
Abstract:
The NJ Division of Fish & Wildlife (DFW), Atlantic Flyway Council, and US Fish and Wildlife Service cooperatively manage migratory game bird hunting seasons. These zones delineate hunting areas for woodcock in New Jersey.
Purpose:
This data can be used to locate and identify woodcock hunting location information.
Supplemental_Information:
For information related to Woodcock Zones and Zone Descriptions, please go to: https://www.njfishandwildlife.com/pdf/huntzones/woodcockzones.pdf.
